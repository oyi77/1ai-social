# Features

1AI Social provides a comprehensive suite of tools for automating and managing your social media presence across multiple platforms.

## Content Generation

### AI-Powered Writing

Generate engaging social media content using advanced AI models:

- **Multiple AI Providers**: Choose between OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude), or other providers
- **Tone Control**: Adjust tone from professional to casual, funny to serious
- **Length Options**: Generate short tweets, medium posts, or long-form content
- **Topic Prompts**: Provide a topic and let AI create relevant content
- **Content Refinement**: Regenerate or edit AI suggestions until perfect

### Content Templates

Save time with pre-built templates:

- Product announcements
- Event promotions
- Motivational quotes
- Industry news commentary
- Behind-the-scenes updates
- Customer testimonials

### Media Support

Enhance posts with rich media:

- **Images**: Upload JPG, PNG, GIF (up to 10MB)
- **Videos**: Upload MP4, MOV (up to 100MB)
- **Multiple Images**: Create carousel posts (Instagram, LinkedIn)
- **Alt Text**: Add accessibility descriptions for images
- **Media Library**: Store and reuse media across posts

## Multi-Platform Posting

### Supported Platforms

Post to multiple platforms simultaneously:

- **X (Twitter)**: Text posts, threads, images, videos, polls
- **Instagram**: Feed posts, Stories, Reels, carousels
- **TikTok**: Video posts with captions and hashtags
- **LinkedIn**: Personal and company page posts, articles
- **Facebook**: Coming soon

### Platform-Specific Optimization

Content automatically adapts to each platform:

- **Character Limits**: Auto-truncate or split content
- **Hashtag Placement**: Optimal positioning per platform
- **Image Sizing**: Resize to platform requirements
- **Video Formats**: Convert to supported formats
- **Link Previews**: Generate rich previews where supported

### Cross-Posting

Post once, publish everywhere:

1. Create content in the editor
2. Select target platforms
3. Preview how it looks on each platform
4. Customize per platform if needed
5. Publish to all at once

## Scheduling and Campaigns

### Post Scheduling

Plan your content calendar:

- **Date and Time Picker**: Schedule posts weeks or months ahead
- **Timezone Support**: Schedule in your local timezone
- **Best Time Suggestions**: AI recommends optimal posting times
- **Queue Management**: View and edit scheduled posts
- **Bulk Scheduling**: Upload CSV to schedule multiple posts

### Campaigns

Organize related content:

- **Campaign Creation**: Group posts by theme, product, or event
- **Recurring Posts**: Set up daily, weekly, or monthly content
- **Campaign Analytics**: Track performance across all campaign posts
- **Templates**: Save campaign structure for reuse
- **Team Collaboration**: Assign campaign tasks to team members

### Content Calendar

Visual planning interface:

- **Monthly View**: See all scheduled posts at a glance
- **Drag and Drop**: Reschedule posts by dragging
- **Color Coding**: Organize by campaign, platform, or status
- **Filters**: View specific platforms or campaigns
- **Export**: Download calendar as PDF or CSV

## Analytics Tracking

### Performance Metrics

Track what matters:

- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares, saves
- **Reach**: Impressions, unique viewers
- **Growth**: Follower count changes
- **Click-Through**: Link clicks and conversions
- **Video Stats**: Views, watch time, completion rate

### Platform-Specific Insights

Deep dive into each platform:

- **X Analytics**: Retweets, quote tweets, profile visits
- **Instagram Insights**: Story views, profile actions, website clicks
- **TikTok Analytics**: Video views, shares, profile views
- **LinkedIn Stats**: Post impressions, engagement rate, demographics

### Reports

Generate comprehensive reports:

- **Date Range Selection**: Daily, weekly, monthly, custom
- **Comparison Views**: Compare periods or campaigns
- **Export Options**: PDF, CSV, or Excel
- **Automated Reports**: Email reports on schedule
- **Custom Dashboards**: Build your own metric views

### AI Insights

Get actionable recommendations:

- **Best Performing Content**: What works for your audience
- **Optimal Posting Times**: When your audience is most active
- **Content Suggestions**: Topics trending in your niche
- **Engagement Predictions**: Forecast post performance

## Webhook Integrations

### Incoming Webhooks

Trigger actions from external systems:

- **Create Posts**: POST to `/api/webhooks/posts`
- **Schedule Content**: Automated scheduling via API
- **Update Campaigns**: Modify existing campaigns
- **Trigger Analytics**: Request performance reports

### Outgoing Webhooks

Get notified of events:

- **Post Published**: When scheduled posts go live
- **Engagement Milestones**: Hit follower or engagement goals
- **Errors**: Failed posts or connection issues
- **Analytics Updates**: Daily or weekly performance summaries

### Integration Examples

Connect with your tools:

- **Zapier**: Trigger posts from Google Sheets, Airtable, etc.
- **Make (Integromat)**: Build complex automation workflows
- **Custom Apps**: Use webhooks in your own applications
- **RSS Feeds**: Auto-post from blog RSS feeds

## Multi-Tenancy

### Organization Management

Built for teams and agencies:

- **Multiple Workspaces**: Separate clients or brands
- **Workspace Switching**: Quick toggle between accounts
- **Isolated Data**: Each workspace has its own content and analytics
- **Branding**: Custom logos and colors per workspace

### Team Collaboration

Work together efficiently:

- **Role-Based Access**: Admin, Editor, Viewer permissions
- **User Invitations**: Email invites with role assignment
- **Activity Logs**: See who did what and when
- **Approval Workflows**: Require review before publishing
- **Comments**: Discuss posts before they go live

### Client Management

Perfect for agencies:

- **Client Accounts**: Manage multiple client workspaces
- **White Label**: Remove 1AI Social branding (Enterprise plan)
- **Client Reporting**: Generate branded reports for clients
- **Access Control**: Limit what clients can see and do
